
UrbanBuzz - Full CRUD Application
Welcome to UrbanBuzz, your go-to platform for sharing and tracking your investment journeys. This full-stack application, powered by MongoDB for the backend and React for the frontend, enables users to create, read, update, and delete social media posts about their investments, whether they're current or from the past.

Features
1. Create Investment Posts
Easily share details about your investments by creating informative posts. Include relevant information such as investment type, amount, and any additional notes.

3. Browse and Track
Explore a feed of investment posts created by fellow users. Stay updated on the latest investment trends and track your own financial journey.

4. Edit or Delete
Manage your posts effortlessly. Edit the details if there are updates or delete posts that are no longer relevant.
